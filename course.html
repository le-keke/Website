Information and Interaction Design Theory and Practice

Introduction

As a corporate lecturer of Alibaba Design, I co-developed a series of courses "Information and Interaction Design" with the International Design Institute of Zhejiang University, and taught it to junior design students of Zhejiang University, which was launched on the free online course platform of Orange Point, with a total of 15,567 people studying it.

A series of courses jointly was also developed with China Academy of Art, "Digital Product and Multimodal Perceptual Design", for junior design students.
